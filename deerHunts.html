Deer Hunt

We guide Archery, Muzzleloader and Rifle hunts. We commonly see as many as 40 to 50 bucks during our 5-day hunting period. In the past we have had 100% success rates. We have seen 160" to 190" bucks, and even larger bucks are possible, there is very good potential with every hunt for a trophy size kill. We hunt on private land, and public land. We can also hunt roadless hunts that are very remote. This can lead to bigger bucks than can be found from more accessible areas. Rifle hunting is confined to two separate units, north and south. Muzzleloader and archery tags can be filled in either area. All of our hunts include a 5-day hunt with one guide per client, lodging in one of our cabins or tents if we are in a remote area, meals, field dressing, and transportation to Vernal, Utah or Grand Junction, Colorado. You will need to provide personal gear, and weapon. Unless you purchase a land owner tag for an additional price, you need to enter the Utah draw. You can apply for Hunts in Utah by clicking apply for hunt below. Contact us to schedule your hunt or for more information.
