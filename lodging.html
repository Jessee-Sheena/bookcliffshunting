Lodging

We have three cabins with nice set ups. The Fishing Cabin is our most modern and was constructed ten years ago. It has nice amenities susch as running water, bathroom, a full Kitchen, and a sleeping area with full size bunks. There is also a covered porch with great views of the four acre fishing pond, and the beautiful canyon meadows Main Canyon. The second cabin, the Bunk House, has been a favorite lodging site for many clients. The Main part of this cabin was built in the 30's and was origionally located about 3 miles down the canyon from where it sits today. A large flood covered the water source for the old homestead so the cabin was moved in the 40's to it's current location. An addition was built onto it to add functionality and it was turned into the Bunk house for the hired help. The old main ranch headquarters is a large cabin with a kitchen, two bedrooms, and a bathroom. This is the oldest and the largest guest cabin on the ranch.
