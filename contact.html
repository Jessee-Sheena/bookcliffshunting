Directions to our Ranch.
Coming from Vernal Take the Seep Ridge road to the Pine spring canyon turn off. Take this road to Main canyon. Take the Main canyon south fork road all the way to the ranch. Coming from I 70 Take Hay canyon road to the Bookcliff divide road. Turn east for a half mile to the Whetrock canyon road. Stay to the right .Take this road all the way to the ranch.
