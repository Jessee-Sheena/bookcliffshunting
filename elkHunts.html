Elk Hunts

The Book Cliffs area is an exceptional trophy elk unit. The size of the area, and difficult terrain can make this unit a very challenging place to hunt. If you decide to hire a guide, we want you to hire the best! We are the best guides in the area. We have excellent elk hunts with about a 99% success rate. We have had bulls between 315 to 380. Larger Elk are possible. We can assure you that rut hunts and post rut hunts are both excellent hunts. All of our hunts consist of 5 days spent with one guide per hunter. They include lodging, meals, field dressing, and transportation to either Vernal, Utah or Grand Junction, Colorado. Give us a call to set up your hunt! 970 640 7281.
