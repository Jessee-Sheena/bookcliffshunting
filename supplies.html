Supply List and Information

Clothing: Wool is one of the best ways to stay warm. Wool is breathable and will absorb around 30% of its weight of water before you will feel wet, while keeping you warm. Lighter weight wool will enable you to dress in layers, thus allowing you to add or remove clothing to maintain ultimate comfort. As it can be quite warm during the day, and cold at night. You may want to keep clothing slightly baggy to allow for air circulation. You want to ensure you have raingear that is large enough to be worn on top of all your layers. Make sure to be aware of how your clothing can attract attention of animals. If the clothing, packs or other accessories are reflective they may flash and draw attention. Also, you need to ensure that you select clothing that is quiet. Denim, flannel, cotton, and wool are excellent choices. Stay away from clothing that rustles as you move.

Boots: Obtain Sturdy boots with good traction, waterproof boots are a good option to help keep feet dry. Wool socks will help keep feet dry and warm.

Hat: Try to find a hat that will offer protection from the sun and from the cold. Sleeping in a tent can be quite cold, having a knit cap can help keep you warm at night.

Gloves: Water proof or water repellent gloves will help to keep your hands warm and dry.

Sleeping Bag: You need a high-quality winter rated sleeping bag.

Personal Needs: bring things that you need on a regular basis such as any medications you need, personal hygiene products, etc. Bring a flashlight, sunscreen, matches, and a small first aid kit. Also bring a few trash bags to stow away garbage and any dirty items. Please make sure that you are in physical condition for rigorous hiking and rough living. These areas we hunt are remote and it can take a long time for medical help to reach us.

Meat bags:Bring 4 meat bags for Elk, 2 for deer or bear.

Scope & binoculars:bring your binoculars and scope to help scout the animals.

Firearms and ammunition:bring the appropriate rifle, bow, muzzleloader for your specific hunt. Bring the proper amount of ammunition.
